3D visualization : a new perspective about your data

3D visualization borehole data helps to better grasp the difference in depth and the layers of your data. Here, you can even move your graph around to study your data from different angles and perspectives.

  • 01 Why is it important to visualize your data in 3D ?

    Soils and sediment are not only considered in their surface but also their depth. Thus, having those layers visualized in 3D is giving a more intiuitive and newer aspect of studying your data.

  • 02 How is the data prepared ?

    Back-end computation with ArchPy plugin will compute, based on your inputs, the corresponding models of borehole maps.

  • 03 What information does one voxel contain ?

    A voxel can be seen as a cube of unit 1 that will contain its position on the x,y and z axis. A voxel will also have a corresponding soil layer attached to it. Indeed, at the same depth, 2 voxels could indicate 2 different types of soil.
